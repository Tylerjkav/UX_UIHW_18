Hello.

Tyler has been working as a graphic designer for the last 14+ years. She was always fascinated and inspired by the possibilities of digital projects over the print medium. So after the COVID-19 pandemic hit, and she lost her job, she decided it was time to make a career change and enrolled in a UI/UI Bootcamp with the University of Arizona.

Tyler aims to create experiences that are easy to use, create delight and serve an important business function, in order to build brand loyalty and trust. She enjoys discovering the problem and working to find solutions.
